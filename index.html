"Marketing Maestros" is a dynamic student club at the Institute of Innovation in Technology and Management. The club serves as a hub for learning and enhancing marketing skills, providing a platform for students to showcase their talents, learn new strategies, and connect with industry professionals. Through a diverse range of events and activities such as social gatherings, reel making competitions, and digital marketing workshops, members gain practical experience and insights into various facets of marketing.
